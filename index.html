Cretaceous Planet: Asset Containment Unit

ACUs (humans) are squares; assets (dinosaurs) are circles.
Click on stuff (in any order) to tell ACUs where to go:
  • ACU + asset: ACU hunts asset.
  • ACU + barracks: Assign ACU to barracks; ACU returns to barracks.
  • barracks + asset: All ACUs assigned to barracks hunt asset.
  • barracks + different barracks: All ACUs assigned to first barracks are assigned and return to second barracks.
  • barracks + same barracks: All ACUs assigned to barracks return there.
  • background: Deselect anything that is selected.
Spacebar pauses to make it easier to click on things.
Click on the squares on the side to hire new ACUs.
